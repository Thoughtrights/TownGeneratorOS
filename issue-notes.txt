does not need to be a bridge.

The land objects are on top of the ocean and river. This makes the
river or orcean appear to not have an edge. This applies to open land,

top of open land, and farm land. It can cut those off. The ocean and
rivers cannot however be on top of town or castles and the town and
castles cannot be on top of rivers or ocean. This is a conflict to
avoid.
